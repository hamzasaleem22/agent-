# AI Image Prompt — Nature’s Beauty (Luxury Cinematic Photorealism)

## Primary Prompt
A breathtaking, ultra-luxury cinematic landscape portraying **nature’s beauty at its most pristine and exclusive**: a crystal-clear alpine lake nestled between dramatic snow-capped peaks and emerald pine forests, foregrounded by dew-kissed wildflowers and smooth river stones; a thin waterfall feeds the lake, creating delicate mist drifting across the water’s surface. **Golden-hour sunrise** breaks over the mountain ridge, casting warm rim light on the treetops and painting long, soft shadows across the meadow; sun rays (god beams) cut through light morning fog, producing subtle volumetric light and a gentle atmospheric haze. The lake reflects the peaks like polished glass with slight ripples near the shoreline; micro-details visible in moss, bark, petals, and wet rock textures.

**Mood & emotion:** serene, awe-inspiring, restorative calm; a sense of rare, untouched wilderness and premium “once-in-a-lifetime” scenery.

**Color palette:** rich emerald greens and deep pine tones, cool glacial blues and teals in the water, warm amber and honey highlights from the sun, soft lavender/rose accents in distant sky; balanced warm–cool contrast with tasteful, natural saturation.

**Materials & textures:** glossy water reflections, matte stone and granite faces, soft velvety moss, crisp needles on conifers, translucent flower petals with subsurface scattering; mist diffusing highlights; subtle sparkle on dew droplets.

**Composition & perspective:** wide-angle cinematic frame (24–28mm), low vantage point with wildflowers in sharp foreground leading lines toward the lake and mountains; strong depth layering (foreground flowers → midground shoreline → background peaks); rule-of-thirds horizon; gentle bokeh in extreme foreground; deep but natural depth of field.

**Lighting & effects:** directional sun from behind ridge, soft fill from sky, high dynamic range, clean specular highlights on water, nuanced shadow detail; faint airborne pollen/particles catching light; minimal lens flare, filmic contrast.

**Style & quality:** photorealistic, hyper-detailed, 8K, HDR, masterpiece-level landscape photography, premium travel editorial aesthetic, pristine clarity, no artifacts.

people, buildings, roads, power lines, trash, haze smog, over-saturation, cartoon, CGI/plastic look, low-res, blurry, muddy details, harsh noise, banding, blown highlights, crushed shadows, excessive lens flare, watermark, text, logo
