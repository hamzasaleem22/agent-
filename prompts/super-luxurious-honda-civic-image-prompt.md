# Super-Luxurious Honda Civic — AI Image Prompt

## Primary Prompt (Photoreal, Cinematic Luxury)
Create an **ultra-premium, super-luxurious Honda Civic** reimagined as a **limited-edition executive performance sedan**, photographed like a high-end automotive campaign.

**Subject & Concept:** A tastefully modified Honda Civic with **bespoke luxury body kit**, widened stance, flush fitment, subtle carbon-fiber aero accents, and premium detailing (no excessive decals). The car should feel **exclusive, handcrafted, and expensive**—like a one-off coachbuilt edition.

**Environment & Setting:** Parked on a **wet, mirror-like black stone driveway** in front of a **modern glass-and-travertine mansion** with soft interior glow. Background includes minimalistic landscaping, a reflecting pool, and distant city skyline lights.

**Mood & Emotion:** **Quiet power, prestige, confidence**—an elite, after-hours showroom vibe.

**Lighting (Cinematic):**
- **Golden-hour transitioning into blue-hour** (warm-to-cool cinematic shift)
- Key light: soft warm sun grazing the body lines from the rear-left
- Fill: cool ambient sky light from the front-right
- Practical lights: subtle mansion interior lighting + low pathway lights
- **Specular highlights** on the paint and chrome; deep, controlled shadows

**Color Palette & Luxury Tone:**
- Primary: **deep obsidian black** or **midnight emerald** paint
- Secondary: **brushed champagne-gold** accents (badging, trim)
- Accents: **cool sapphire reflections** from sky and city lights
- Overall: high-contrast, premium, clean, non-neon

**Materials & Textures:**
- Paint: multi-layer **high-gloss ceramic-coated** finish with crisp reflections
- Trim: **brushed metal**, minimal chrome, and satin elements
- Aero: subtle **matte carbon fiber weave** visible in close highlights
- Wheels: forged multi-spoke with **satin champagne** finish
- Glass: slightly tinted, perfectly clean
- Ground: wet polished stone with realistic puddle edges and reflections

**Material–Light Interaction:** Sharp specular reflections along hood and fenders, soft roll-off on curved panels, clean rim-light outlining silhouette, realistic Fresnel reflections on windows, micro-sparkle in metallic paint under grazing light.

**Perspective & Composition:**
- Camera: **low 3/4 front angle**, 35mm lens look
- Composition: car centered with slight dynamic lead space, mansion as elegant backdrop
- Depth of field: moderately shallow (car razor sharp, background gently softened)
- Framing: cinematic wide (avoid distortion), symmetrical architectural lines

**Style & Aesthetic:** **Photorealistic luxury automotive advertisement**, modern, minimal, premium, tasteful—no cartoon look.

**Detail Level & Visual Quality:** hyper-detailed, ultra-realistic, HDR-balanced, crisp micro-contrast, 8K, masterpiece, clean post-production, no artifacts.

**Rarity & Value Cues:** bespoke coachbuilt cues, discreet limited-edition badge, perfect panel gaps, premium craftsmanship, exclusive trim package, immaculate finish.

**Atmosphere & Visual Effects:** subtle ground mist, faint volumetric light near mansion entrance, soft bokeh from distant city lights, light rain residue on lower panels, gentle depth haze.

**Negative Prompt / Avoid:**
Avoid: cartoon, anime, low-res, over-saturated neon, heavy decals, extreme camber, mismatched body panels, dirty paint, crowded background, illegible logos, warped wheels, distorted proportions, text overlays, watermark.

## Suggested Settings
- Aspect ratio: **16:9** (cinematic) or **4:5** (editorial)
- Style: photoreal / cinematic
- Quality: high / ultra / 8k
- If supported: add **"ray-traced reflections"**, **"global illumination"**, **"film grain (subtle)"**
