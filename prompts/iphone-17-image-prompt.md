# AI Image Prompt — iPhone 17 (Luxury Cinematic Product Shot)

## Primary Prompt (copy/paste)
Create a **masterpiece, ultra-photorealistic luxury product photograph** of the **“iPhone 17” concept smartphone** as the hero subject. The device features an **edge-to-edge display with ultra-thin bezels**, a **precision-machined titanium frame**, a **frosted matte glass back**, and a **minimal, refined camera array** with sapphire lens covers. Present it as an **exclusive, limited-edition flagship** with impeccable craftsmanship.

### Subject & Concept
- Single iPhone 17 as the centerpiece, premium industrial design, flawless fit-and-finish
- Subtle branding only (no large logos), emphasis on form, materials, and engineering

### Environment & Setting
- High-end studio set: **dark basalt stone pedestal** on a **polished black marble surface**
- Background: **soft charcoal-to-midnight gradient** with faint architectural lines suggesting a modern luxury showroom
- Sparse, elegant props only: a thin sheet of brushed metal and a small prism-like glass accent (optional)

### Mood & Emotion
- **Quiet power, exclusivity, precision, future-forward elegance**
- Calm, premium, editorial tone (like a luxury watch campaign)

### Lighting (cinematic, premium)
- **Key light**: large softbox from upper-left, soft but defined highlights along the titanium edge
- **Rim light**: narrow strip light from rear-right to carve the silhouette and camera bump
- **Fill**: subtle bounce to preserve shadow detail without flattening
- Add **micro-specular highlights** on chamfers, **controlled reflections** on glass, deep clean shadows

### Color Palette & Luxury Tone
- Primary: **graphite / midnight black**
- Secondary: **cool titanium silver**
- Accents: **deep blue-violet** reflection band and a faint **warm amber** edge glint for contrast
- Overall: cool, high-end, minimal, modern

### Materials, Textures & Light Interaction
- Titanium frame: fine brushed grain, crisp chamfers, realistic anisotropic reflection
- Back glass: frosted matte diffusion with soft bloom on highlights
- Screen: pristine glossy glass, subtle anti-reflective coating, faint UI glow (minimal)
- Marble: glossy with realistic veining, sharp reflections near the base
- Basalt pedestal: porous, matte texture with gentle falloff

### Perspective & Composition
- **Three-quarter hero angle** (slightly low camera height), phone tilted 10–15° for dynamic presence
- Composition: centered with **premium negative space**, rule-of-thirds highlights
- **Shallow depth of field**: phone tack-sharp, background softly defocused
- Lens look: 85mm equivalent, clean optical clarity

### Detail Level & Visual Quality
- **Photorealistic, hyper-detailed, 8K, studio-grade product photography**
- Perfect edges, no dust, no fingerprints, no distortion, accurate reflections

### Atmosphere & Visual Effects
- Subtle volumetric haze for depth separation
- Fine bokeh highlights in the far background (very minimal)
- Soft vignette, gentle contrast curve, luxury editorial color grade

### Story & Context
- Implies a launch reveal in an elite showroom: the newest premium device, engineered perfection

### Output Requirements
- 1:1 or 4:5 vertical campaign frame
- No text overlays, no watermarks, no hands

## Negative Prompt
cartoon, illustration, low-poly, CGI-looking plastic, cheap materials, noisy image, oversharpening, heavy bloom, messy reflections, fingerprints, dust, scratches, warped perspective, wide-angle distortion, cluttered background, excessive UI, text, watermark, logo-heavy branding, artifacts, banding, low resolution

- Style: photoreal / product photography / cinematic
- Quality: high / ultra
- DoF: shallow
- Lighting: studio softbox + rim
- Seed: fixed for consistency across variants
